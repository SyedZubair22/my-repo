My favorirte movies of all time

Avengers:Endgame


The film features grand, visually stunning battle scenes and a complex storyline. It provides satisfying conclusions and significant growth for many characters. The movie is filled with callbacks to previous MCU movies and moments that pay homage to the journey of the characters and fans.

Spiderman:No way home


The movie is packed with action, emotional moments, and nostalgic references for long-time Spider-Man fans. It’s a thrilling ride that explores the consequences of Peter’s choices and the importance of his role as Spider-Man.

3 idiots


3 Idiots” is a popular Indian Hindi-language comedy-drama film released in 2009. Directed by Rajkumar Hirani and based on Chetan Bhagat’s novel “Five Point Someone,” the movie stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, and Boman Irani.
